About


Isaac is an extremely outgoing and friendly boy who loves to greet all he meets. He has early indications of being the life of the party, but is also generally quite pleased in a quieter setting with a good book. Being the youngest among three brothers, Isaac has learned tenacity at a young age. He is very willing to enter confrontation via screams and full out tackles, to ensure that he is not steamrolled by his older brothers. Even in these times however, Isaac's displays an unusual amount self-awareness and empathy towards his rivals. Bottom line is that Isaac is a dream addition to Team Weiler.
